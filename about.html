Video Sync

Watch videos in sync with your friends

About the Project

Video Sync is a browser extension that allows you to synchronize video playback across multiple browsers. Whether you want to watch a movie with friends remotely or stay in sync during a presentation, Video Sync makes it simple and seamless.

Key Features

Real-time synchronization - Play, pause, and seek events are synchronized across all connected viewers
Easy sharing - Generate a simple link to share your viewing session with others
Built-in chat - Communicate with other viewers without leaving the video page
Support for multiple sites - Works with YouTube and many other video platforms

How to Use

  1. Navigate to any page with a video
  2. The Video Sync panel will appear on the page
  3. Share the generated link with friends
  4. When they open the link, your videos will be synchronized

You can also create a new room by clicking "Create New Room" in the extension popup.

Technical Details

Video Sync uses WebSocket technology to provide real-time communication between browsers. The extension monitors video playback events and transmits them to all connected clients through a central server.

The project is built using JavaScript and Chrome Extension APIs.
