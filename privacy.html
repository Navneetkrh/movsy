Movsy - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Movsy ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our browser extension. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you use Movsy, we collect the following information:

  • Username: A username you choose or that is automatically generated if you don't provide one.
  • Room Information: Identifiers for rooms you create or join.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send in sync rooms.
  • Video Playback Data: Timestamps and playback status to enable synchronization.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To enable video synchronization between different users
  • To facilitate real-time chat in sync rooms
  • To identify you to other users in the sync room
  • To restore your chat history when you rejoin a room

Data Storage

We store information in the following ways:

  • Local Storage: Chat history and room information are stored in your browser's local storage.
  • Sync Storage: User preferences like your username and server URL are stored in Chrome sync storage.
  • Server Storage: Temporary information for real-time communication is stored on our server while a session is active.

Chat history is limited to the most recent 100 messages per room and is only accessible to users who have joined that room.

External Services

The extension connects to our WebSocket server (ws://movsy-production.up.railway.app) to facilitate real-time communication between users. No personal data is shared with third parties beyond what is necessary for the functionality of the extension.

Data Retention

Chat history and room data are stored locally in your browser. We do not permanently store this data on our servers. Data on our servers is temporarily held only for the duration of active sessions and is deleted when all users leave a room.

Your Rights

You can clear your chat history and stored data at any time by clearing your browser data. You can also choose to use the extension without providing a custom username.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension.
